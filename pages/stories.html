Personal stories are written by Indigenous people on their overcoming of mental health issues. These Stories are here to help you in your journey

  • Jitendra K.

    Sarah started feeling overwhelmed by life's demands. She struggled to find joy in the things she once loved, and a persistent cloud of sadness engulfed her. Anxiety crept into her daily life, making even the simplest tasks feel like insurmountable challenges. She couldn't sleep, and her self-esteem plummeted. It was as if she was in a constant battle with her own mind..

  • Karan ahuja

    Therapy helped Sarah understand the root causes of her struggles. She learned to identify and manage her anxiety triggers. Slowly, she regained her sense of self-worth and started finding joy in life again. The therapist taught her coping strategies and mindfulness techniques that became her allies in the ongoing battle against her mental health issues.
